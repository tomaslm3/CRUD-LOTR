Lord of The Rings

Proyecto CRUD con mongoDB

Funciones implementadas

  • Crear Razas y Personajes
  • Elegir la raza de cada personaje
  • Llamar a las razas y personajes por: Nombre, ID o todos
  • Editar Razas y/o Personajes
  • Eliminar Razas y/o Personajes

Ejemplos flags:

    Razas:

  • Crear Raza:
    POST http://localhost:8080/race/create
    body: { "name": "Elves", "origins": "Created by Iluvatar, being first of his Children", "languages": ["Quenya", "Sindarin"], "lifespan": "Immortal", "height": "Taller than average height of Mankid", "skinColor": ["Pale complexion"], "hairColor": ["Black", "Silver", "Gold", "Blonde"], "weaponry": ["Swords", "Bows"] }

  • Llamar Razas:
    GET http://localhost:8080/races

  • Llamar Raza por nombre:
    GET http://localhost:8080/races?name={name}

  • Llamar Raza por id:
    GET http://localhost:8080/race/{ID}

  • Editar Raza:
    PUT http://localhost:8080/race/update/{ID}
    body: { "name": "Men", "origins": "Created by Iluvatar, bear the Gift of Men, which is mortality", "languages": ["Westron", "dunaic", "Rohirric", "Dalish", "Black Speech"], "lifespan": "80-500 based in their culture", "height": "Taller than average height of Mankid", "skinColor": ["variable based in their culture"], "hairColor": ["Black", "Silver", "Gold", "Blonde", "Brown"], "weaponry": ["Swords", "Bows", "Axes", "Polearms", "Spears", "Maces", "Daggers", "Whips", "Hammers", "Flails", "Sling"] }

  • Eliminar Raza:
    DELETE http://localhost:8080/race/delete/{ID}

    Personajes:

  • Crear Personaje:
    POST http://localhost:8080/character/create
    body: { "name": "Glorfindel", "titles": ["Lord of the Golden Flower"], "birth": "Possibly Year of Trees", "death": "First Age 510", "hair": "Golden", "race": "Elves" }

  • Llamar Personajes:
    GET http://localhost:8080/characters

  • Llamar Personaje por nombre:
    GET http://localhost:8080/characters?name={name}

  • Llamar Personaje por id:
    GET http://localhost:8080/character/{ID}

  • Editar Personaje:
    PUT http://localhost:8080/character/update/{ID}
    body: { "name": "Fingolfin", "titles": ["High King of the Noldor", "King of Hithlum"], "birth": "Year of Trees 1190", "death": "First Age 456", "hair": "Dark", }

  • Eliminar Personaje:
    DELETE http://localhost:8080/character/delete/{ID}
